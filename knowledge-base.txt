=== DATA COLLECTION SEQUENCE ===

Personal Details (ask one at a time):
1. Full name
2. Age / Date of birth
3. Gender
4. City / Location
5. Phone number
6. Email address
7. Occupation / Profession
8. Annual income range (Below 5L / 5-10L / 10-25L / 25-50L / Above 50L)
9. Marital status
10. Number of dependents / family members to cover

Insurance Screening (ask sensitively, frame as standard process):
11. Existing insurance policies? Which type?
12. Pre-existing medical conditions? (diabetes, hypertension, heart conditions, asthma, thyroid)
13. Tobacco/smoking use?
14. Regular alcohol consumption?
15. Hospitalization in last 4 years? If yes, reason?
